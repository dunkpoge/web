How It Works

How to Earn $POGE

01

Mint a Dunk Poge NFT

Mint one or more of the 10,000 on-chain NFTs at 0.005 ETH each. Max 10 per wallet.

02

Stake Your NFT

Approve and stake your NFT(s) in the immutable staking contract. No lock-up period — unstake anytime.

03

Earn $POGE

Rewards accumulate every second. Claim anytime, or they are automatically sent when you unstake.

04

Build Your Multiplier

The longer you stake continuously, the more you earn. Multiplier grows from 1x → 2x over 180 days.

Emission Schedule

Reward Decay

Rewards decay quadratically over 264 years. Early stakers earn significantly more. The loyalty multiplier partially offsets the decay for long-term holders.

Time Base Rate (per NFT/day) With 2x Multiplier
Day 1~10 POGE~10 POGE
Day 180~6.25 POGE~9.375 POGE
Day 365~3.75 POGE~7.5 POGE
Day 730~1 POGE~2 POGE

Loyalty multiplier grows from 1x to 2x over 180 days of continuous staking. Claiming rewards does not reset the multiplier — only unstaking does.

Security

Trustless Guarantees

No Admin Functions

The staking contract has zero admin functions. It cannot be paused, upgraded, or modified by anyone.

No Mint Function

POGE supply is fixed at 1 billion. No one can mint additional tokens after deployment.

Always Withdrawable

Your NFTs are always withdrawable via emergencyWithdraw() even if the reward pool is depleted.

No Vesting, No Cliffs

There are no team tokens, no vesting schedules, no cliff unlocks. 100% of supply goes to stakers.

Verifiable On-Chain

Emission rates, decay curves, and multipliers are public contract constants. Anyone can verify.

Censorship Resistant

No admin can prevent claiming rewards or unstaking. The contract runs autonomously forever.
